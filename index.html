About Us

Fleas Asphalt is more than just a brand — it’s a lifestyle inspired by bold streetwear culture and the passion for customization. For trendsetters like Gen Z, Alpha, and Beta, we deliver stylish apparel and accessories that combine comfort, individuality, and cutting-edge trends. From graphic tees to custom merchandise, Fleas Asphalt lets you express yourself with confidence: Live bold, Be unique, Wear Fleas Asphalt!

Meanwhile, for lowrider enthusiasts, Onthel Lowrider Parts provides premium spare parts for modifying or restoring your cherished bikes. From frames and tires to unique accessories, we support your creativity in crafting iconic and stylish lowrider bikes. Discover everything you need for bold self-expression—whether it’s on the streets or on two wheels. Stay bold, stay iconic, with Fleas Asphalt and Onthel Lowrider Parts!
